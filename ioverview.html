🇮🇳 India - Country Overview

India, the world's largest democracy, is a culturally rich and diverse country in South Asia. It is known for its ancient history, spiritual traditions, colorful festivals, and flavorful cuisine. From the Himalayan mountains to the coastal beaches, India offers a stunning geographical variety. It is home to over 1.4 billion people who speak hundreds of languages and follow a multitude of religions. India’s economy is rapidly growing, and its contributions in technology, space, arts, and cuisine are celebrated globally.

India is also globally famous for its food, from spicy curries to street snacks like Golgappe. Its unity in diversity reflects in every aspect of Indian life — culture, language, dress, and cuisine.
