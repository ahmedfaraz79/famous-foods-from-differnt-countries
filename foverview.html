🇫🇷 Overview of French Cuisine

French cuisine is famous worldwide for its flavor, tradition, and culinary elegance. From rich sauces to buttery pastries, it reflects centuries of mastery. French food culture emphasizes fresh ingredients, fine technique, and regional diversity.

From Parisian cafés serving croissants and coffee to countryside tables offering duck confit and aged cheeses, French food celebrates both simplicity and sophistication.
