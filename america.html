🇺🇸 Classic American Burger

American cuisine is known for its variety and hearty flavors. The classic cheeseburger is a true icon — a juicy beef patty sandwiched between toasted buns, topped with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and sauces. This dish represents fast food culture and backyard cookouts across the United States.

🧂 Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1/4 lb per patty)
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  • Step 1: Season ground beef and form patties.
  • Step 2: Cook patties on grill or skillet (3–4 mins per side).
  • Step 3: Add cheese slice during last 30 seconds of cooking.
  • Step 4: Toast buns lightly.
  • Step 5: Assemble: bun, lettuce, patty with cheese, tomato, onion, condiments, top bun.
