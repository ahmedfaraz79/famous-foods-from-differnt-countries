Italian Pizza Overview

Pizza is one of Italy's most iconic dishes, known worldwide for its thin crust, rich tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella cheese. Originating from Naples, traditional Italian pizzas are baked in wood-fired ovens and topped with simple, fresh ingredients like basil, tomatoes, and olive oil. Whether it's a classic Margherita or a gourmet variant, pizza represents the heart of Italian street food and culinary pride.
