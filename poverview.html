Pakistan is a South Asian country known for its breathtaking natural landscapes, ancient civilizations, and deep cultural heritage. From the towering peaks of the Himalayas in the north to the fertile plains of Punjab and the coastal beauty of Karachi in the south, Pakistan offers a rich tapestry of traditions, languages, and lifestyles. It is home to historical sites like Mohenjo-Daro, vibrant festivals, and a people known for their hospitality and resilience. With Urdu as its national language and Islam as its major religion, Pakistan stands as a proud, diverse nation with a bright cultural identity.
