Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that has become a global culinary icon. It consists of vinegared rice paired with various ingredients such as raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits. Sushi showcases the balance, simplicity, and attention to detail that defines Japanese cuisine.
