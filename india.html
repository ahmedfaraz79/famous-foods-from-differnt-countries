Golgappe, also known as Pani Puri or Puchka, is one of the most iconic street foods in India. These crispy, hollow puris are filled with spicy and tangy flavored water, mashed potatoes, chickpeas, and a mix of chutneys. Every region in India has its own twist, making it a favorite snack for all ages.

  • Step 2: Mix green masala with tamarind pulp, cumin, chaat masala, salt, and cold water.
  • Step 3: Mix mashed potatoes and chickpeas with salt and chaat masala in a bowl.
  • Step 4: Crack the top of each puri, add potato mix, then pour spiced water.
  • Step 5: Optionally add sweet chutney. Serve immediately!
