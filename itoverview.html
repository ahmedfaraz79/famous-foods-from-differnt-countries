Italy Overview

Italy is a country in Southern Europe known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and diverse landscapes — from the snowy Alps to Mediterranean coastlines. It has been the birthplace of ancient civilizations, Renaissance art, and cultural movements that shaped the world. Italy is also celebrated for its vibrant traditions, fashion, and deep influence on global cuisine and lifestyle.
