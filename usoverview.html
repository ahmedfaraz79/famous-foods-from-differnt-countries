🇺🇸 Overview of American Cuisine

American cuisine is a melting pot of global flavors, influenced by the diverse immigrant cultures that built the nation. From fast food icons like burgers and hot dogs to traditional Thanksgiving feasts, the food reflects the values of convenience, comfort, and celebration.

Each region in the USA has its specialties: Southern fried chicken, New England clam chowder, Tex-Mex tacos, and Hawaiian poke bowls. American food culture continues to evolve with innovation, fusion, and global inspiration.
